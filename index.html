About Gucci Mane

Gucci Mane, also known as Radric Delantic Davis is a man of many loves. He is a connoisseur of luxury cars, rare jewelry, and clever words. Throughout his career as a rapper he has released a multitude of albums and jail time to boast in those songs. Gucci Mane lives a fast paced life with tons of pass times funded by his net worth and clout on the streets. No matter where you live I would say Gucci is a household name. But he would not have had his famous 1017 records without the help of his buddies T.I. and Young Jeezy.

What people believe he means in his music

Mr. Davis has release a total of twelve studio albums and over 70 mixtapes. His music, while assumed to be simple and similar has a deeper meaning. It does not simply reiterate the fast life of a rapper and the materialistic values that money can bring you. It delves into the ideas of the hustle and why you cannot trust anyone in the streets.

What he means to me and why I like him

Radric is not just a rapper in my mind, but a lyrical mogul. His rap is an art that few actually can consume seriously. His melodies and lyric combined with rapid ad-lib use and top tier features such as Migos, Travis Scott, and Lil Pump further his status quo. Understandably, you can assume that Mr. Davis is a favorite for me. One reason is his because his grind is impeccable. The second reason is because I am from Atlanta like him and you have to route for your people.
